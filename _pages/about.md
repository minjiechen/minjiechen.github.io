We develop future power conversion systems at Princeton. We aim at making fundamental breakthroughs in power electronics to enable important and emerging applications.

Our research focuses include:
- Smarter power electronics at the grid edge
- Smaller power electronics for robotics and EVs
- Ultra-Efficient power electronics for information and data systems
- Design methods and software tools for power electronics and system architectures
